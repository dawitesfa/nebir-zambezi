About Tiger Zambezi

Pioneering the future of sustainable mining, powering a prosperous Africa.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to redefine resource extraction, Tiger Zambezi is a leading mining company with a steadfast commitment to sustainable and responsible operations. We believe that mining can and must be a force for good, unearthing the essential minerals that drive global progress while upholding the highest standards of environmental stewardship and community engagement. Our journey began in the heart of Africa, and we are dedicated to creating lasting value for our stakeholders, from our employees and local communities to our investors.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We are committed to minimizing our environmental footprint through advanced land reclamation, efficient water management, and reduced carbon emissions.

Safety

The well-being of our team is our top priority. We maintain a zero-harm culture with rigorous safety protocols and continuous training programs.

Community

We are dedicated partners in development, contributing to local infrastructure, creating jobs, and investing in the communities where we operate.

Our History

2005: Founding

Tiger Zambezi is founded with an initial focus on copper exploration in the Zambezi region.

2012: Mine Operations

Construction is completed, and the first major mining operations begin, utilizing state-of-the-art machinery.

2020: Sustainable Growth

We achieve a new benchmark in sustainability, implementing a company-wide initiative for carbon reduction and land reclamation.
